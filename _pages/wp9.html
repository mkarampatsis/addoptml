Working Packages

1 Work package number: WP9
2 Work package title: Management and coordination
3 Starting Month: 1/48
4 Lead Beneficiary: NTUA
5 Objectives: In this WP the administrative and financial obligations of the consortium will be performed. These include coordination of the project, reporting, supervision and intra-consortium communication support.
6 Description of Work:

NTUA beneficiary, as the ADDOPTML project coordination institution (CI), will administratively supervise all documentation and reports to the EC with the help of the WPs and Tasks Leaders.

  • Task 9.1. Kick-off meeting open to the scientific community, (Lead by NTUA).
  • Task 9.2. Mid-term meeting open to the scientific community, (Lead by NTUA).
  • Task 9.3. Final meeting open to the scientific community, (Lead by NTUA).
  • Task 9.4. Coordination and Management open to the scientific community, (Lead by NTUA). The contribution of beneficiaries and partners: all contributors will participate in line with the research work plan, provide timely reporting on activities, results and financials and take part in communication and events where needed.
